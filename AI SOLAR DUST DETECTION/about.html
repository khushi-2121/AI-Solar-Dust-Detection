About Our Project

AI Solar Monitoring System is an intelligent solution designed to improve the efficiency and lifespan of solar panels by detecting dust accumulation and environmental conditions in real time.

Dust, pollution, and weather conditions significantly reduce solar energy generation. Our system continuously monitors solar panel performance, detects efficiency drops using AI logic, and automatically notifies users when cleaning is required.

Why This System Is Important?

  • Dust can reduce solar panel efficiency by up to 30–40%.
  • Manual inspection is time-consuming and inefficient.
  • Missed cleaning leads to long-term energy and financial losses.

Key Features

  • AI-based dust detection and cleaning prediction
  • Live location and temperature monitoring
  • Automated cleaning control (Start / Stop)
  • Energy generation and loss estimation
  • Cost impact analysis on electricity bills
  • Real-time dashboard with smart alerts

How It Works

Sensors installed on solar panels collect data such as dust level, sunlight intensity, and temperature. This data is processed using AI algorithms to predict efficiency loss and cleaning requirements.

The system then sends notifications to the user and allows remote control of the cleaning mechanism through the web dashboard.

Future Scope

  • Mobile application support
  • AI-based energy prediction graphs
  • Cloud data storage and analytics
